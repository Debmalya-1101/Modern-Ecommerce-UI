Application shell

Responsive structure before feature complexity

This starter view focuses on layout only: header, sidebar, footer, and a flexible main content area that can hold future pages.

Header

The top bar keeps navigation simple and gives the app a stable brand area on every screen size.

Sidebar

The sidebar stays visible on larger screens and becomes a slide-over drawer on mobile screens.

Main content

This area is powered by the Angular router so future pages can appear here without changing the shell layout.

Why this shell exists

  • It creates a professional starting layout without business logic.
  • It keeps the structure scalable for future route-based pages.
  • It introduces Angular Material navigation in a beginner-readable way.
